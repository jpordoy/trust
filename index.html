Welcome to TRUST Telerehabilitation

We are your one stop teleconsultation and digital rehabilitation service provided by a UK based team of multidisciplinary spinal cord injury clinical specialists.

How Can We Help?

Our multi-disciplinary team of highly specialist professionals are available to provide advice and support regarding all aspects of living with a spinal cord injury. We will a provide plan to assist you with your condition and wherever appropriate make onward referral to our network of specialists working in the field of spinal cord injury.

Our Doctors

Teleconsultation

We offer a consultation with one of our single specialists or our multidisciplinary team via video conferencing

Injury Assessment and Review

Injury Assessment and Review

We provide a comprehensive assessment service conducted by a multidisciplinary team, tailored to your needs. Our highly specialist experts can provide a holistic review of any issues and concerns you have regarding your wellbeing following spinal cord injury.

Treatment Plans

Treatment Plans

Following our assessment our team will provide you with advice and appropriate treatment plan.
